Welcome to the Leave Management System

Our system allows employees to easily request and track their leaves, and managers to approve or reject those requests.

Features

  • Easy to Use

    Our system is designed to be intuitive and easy to use, even for those who are not tech-savvy.

  • Track Leaves

    Employees can easily track their remaining leaves and see how many they have taken and how many they have left.

  • Request and Approve Leaves

    Employees can request leaves and managers can approve or reject those requests, all within the system.

  • Notifications

    Both employees and managers will receive notifications when a leave request is made or approved/rejected.
