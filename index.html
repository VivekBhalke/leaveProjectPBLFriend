  • Easy to Use

    Our system is designed to be intuitive and easy to use, even for those who are not tech-savvy.

  • Track Leaves

    Students can easily track their remaining leaves and see how many they have taken and how many they have left.

    Students can request leaves and managers can approve or reject those requests, all within the system.
